Victoria Vesna

Vesna is a digital media artist and professor at UCLA's digital media arts department. She creates internet, computer, and video art, many of which are interactive and relate to feminism. She has been creating these works since the early 1980s. She is known to engage in experimental creative research between mediums relating to art and technology. Using her art she evaluates how technology relating to communication can affect groups and how scientific innovation can change perception.

By Maggie Galletti
